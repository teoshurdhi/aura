Questions

FAQ

Are these the original designer perfumes?

No — and we're upfront about that. AURA fragrances are our own creations inspired by famous scent profiles, made with quality oils and sold in our own bottles at a fraction of designer prices. We are not affiliated with the brands that inspire us.

How do I pay?

Cash on delivery. You order online, we confirm by phone, and you pay the courier in cash when your package arrives. No card or prepayment needed.

How long does delivery take?

Usually 1–3 working days across Albania. We'll confirm the exact timing when we call you after your order.

Can I check the product before paying?

Yes — inspect the sealed package when the courier arrives. If something is wrong, don't accept it.

Is the fragrance unisex?

AURA is designed to be worn by anyone — a dark amber-spice signature rather than a "his" or "hers" scent.
